The Experience

See more. Effort less. Smile more.

This is snorkelling with a boost: you'll use easy-to-handle scooters (DPVs) to explore coastline and shallow-water scenery with a guide—at a pace that's fun, controlled, and beginner-friendly.

Perfect for:

  • Active travellers who want something unique
  • Couples and friends
  • Non-diving partners who still want a real sea adventure
  • Anyone who loves the sea and wants a "wow" experience

How It Works

Simple by design.

01

Meet the boat

Arrive, gear up, and get comfortable.

02

Briefing on board (during transit)

We explain DPV basics, safe spacing, what to do if you stop, and simple equalisation tips.

03

Guided water session (~2 hours)

We snorkel and glide as a group, with clear boundaries and support.

04

Back on board

Relax, dry off, and enjoy the ride back.

Safety First

We keep it exciting — and structured.

  • Small groups for better control and service
  • Clear rules and a practical briefing
  • Visibility buoys for every guest
  • Routes chosen for manageable traffic and conditions
  • Weather-dependent planning (we don't force bad days)

Requirements

To join, you need:

  • Basic confidence swimming in open water
  • Comfort wearing a mask/snorkel and floating at the surface
  • Reasonable stamina (no athlete required)
  • No significant medical issues that could create risk in open water

If you're unsure, message us — we'll help you decide quickly.

FAQ

Quick answers.

Do I need scuba experience?

No. This is snorkelling at the surface with a guided group.

Will I go deep?

No — this is designed to stay shallow and controlled. We also use swim buoys to support safe limits.

What if the weather is bad?

Safety first: the trip is weather-dependent. If conditions aren't right, we'll reschedule or apply the policy shown at booking.

Can I come if my partner is diving?

Yes — this is ideal for non-diving partners who want a real ocean experience.

What should I bring?

Swimwear, rashguard or snorkeling suit (wetsuit), towel, sun protection, and water. We provide the gear.
